The Ladybird Game

How to Play the Game:

1. For StepUp to press Arrow Up key.

2. For StepDown to press Arrow Down key.

3. For StepRight to press Arrow Right key.

4. For StepLeft to press Arrow Left key.

5. For Turn Clockwise to press "]" key.

6. For Turn AntiClockwise to press "[" key.

7. For Return starting position of ladybird press "H" or "h" key.
